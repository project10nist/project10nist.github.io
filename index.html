Why

Here's why I started this learning log:

  • ——To share my experience and the choices I make along the way of my self-taught education in IT.
  • ——To connect with other learners, from the newbies (like me) to more experienced tech people from all over the world.

By structuring this experience, I can have a panoramic view of what I'm doing, since it’s encompassing lots of resources from a few different places. And by sharing it, I hope to contribute to other learners like me.
